Common Pest Problems

Nairobi Pest Problems We Solve Daily

Nairobi's climate and urban density create ideal conditions for pest infestations. Here are the most common problems our clients face — and how we solve them.

🛏️

Bed Bug Infestations

Spread through hotels, guest houses, and second-hand furniture. They hide in mattress seams and headboards, biting at night. Our heat and chemical combination treatment eliminates them completely.

🪳

Cockroach Outbreaks

German and American cockroaches thrive in Nairobi kitchens and drains. They carry Salmonella and E.coli, threatening food safety compliance for restaurants and food businesses.

🐀

Rat & Mouse Infestations

Rodents gnaw through wiring, contaminate food supplies, and burrow into walls. They pose a serious fire risk and health hazard, especially in warehouses and food storage facilities.

🪵

Termite Damage

Subterranean termites are common in Nairobi homes. They destroy wooden beams, doors, and furniture silently. Early detection and soil treatment prevent costly structural damage.

🦟

Mosquito Breeding

Stagnant water in estates, gardens, and construction sites breeds mosquitoes rapidly. Our larvicide and fogging programs dramatically reduce mosquito populations in large properties.

🐜

Ant Invasions

Black and red ants contaminate food and are persistent in kitchens and gardens. We use colony-targeting baiting systems that eliminate the entire colony, not just the visible workers.
